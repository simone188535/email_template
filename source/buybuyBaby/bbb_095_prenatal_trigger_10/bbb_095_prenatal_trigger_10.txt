It's tough seeing your baby uncomfortable. There are a few issues that nearly every baby (and new parents) go through in the first few months. Don't worry. These things happen, but following the steps below can help minimize your baby's discomfort.

touble sleeping
• Rock your baby in a gentle swing
• Walk your baby in a swing or carrier
• Make sure your baby's room is dark (try room darkening shades)
• Use a sound machine to decrease background noises
• A pacifier can help your baby self-soothe

diaper rash
• Change diapers more frequently
• Completely dry area before putting on oinment or diapers
• Try some diaper-free time
• Add backing soda to your baby's bath water

the sniffles
• Keep the air moist with a humidifier
• Suction out mucus
• Have your baby sleep at a sligh angle (try a crib wedge under your baby's mattress)
• Use saline wipes for quick and gentle relief
